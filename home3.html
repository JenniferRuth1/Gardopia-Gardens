About

Gardopia Gardens is a 501(c)3 nonprofit agency based in San Antonio, founded in May 2015 to address the growing epidemic of obesity-related diseases and environmental issues.

We aim to reduce obesity and malnutrition by increasing accessibility to fresh produce and implementing strategic intervention programming in schools, businesses, and community organizations.

Gardopia Gardens also provides programs and services to educational, commercial, and residential communities, providing tools, materials, and best practices to ensure their sustainability.

Mission

Growing healthy communities through garden-based learning. We have a vision of a sustainable society, empowered and educated to lead healthy lifestyles through gardening. We value health, education, and environment.
